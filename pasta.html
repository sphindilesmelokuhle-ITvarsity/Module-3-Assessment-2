SITHELO'S RESTAURANT

Pasta

Time:1 hr

Yield:2

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Prepare ROYCO® sauce as per instructions on pack. Heat oil & stir-fry courgettes & tomatoes.
  2. Add garlic, salt & pepper.
  3. Remove from heat & stir in the parsley, cooked penne, prepared sauce & eggs in that order.
  4. Return to stove & gently cook frittata without stirring, for 5-10 minutes (until it starts to set).
  5. Sprinkle with the grated cheese & paprika.
  6. Grill until cheese is melted.
  7. Cool slightly, cut into wedges & carefully remove from pan.
