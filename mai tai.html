SITHELO'S RESTAURANT

Mai Tai

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Combine pineapple juice, rum, lime juice, Curaçao, orgeat, and cherry juice in a cocktail shaker. Fill with ice. Shake vigorously for 20 seconds. Strain into an ice-filled rocks glass. Top with dark rum, and garnish with mint, cherry, and pineapple.
