Cheese Cake

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 mins

Yield: 2

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Heat some of the yogurt and add the Pannamousse.
  2. Crush the biscuits and mix them with the malted butter.
  3. Tip into the tin and chill. Whip the soft cheese with the sugar and fold in the semi whipped cream.
  4. Add the yogurt with the Pannamousse to the other part of yogurt and mix togheter with the cheese, the sugar and the cream. Pour the cream onto the biscuits and chill.
  5. Garnish with Amarena Fabbri.
